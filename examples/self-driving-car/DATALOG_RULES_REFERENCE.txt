Head :- Body.

Head      = Conclusion (what is inferred if body is true)
Body      = Conditions (facts that must be true)
:-        = "IF" (reads as "is true if")
,         = AND (all conditions must be true)
Variables = Uppercase letters (V, P, TL, Z, S, L, etc.)

Example:
   critical(V) :- pedestrian(P), inCrosswalk(P), confidence(P, C), C > 0.9.
   
   Reads as:
   "Vehicle V is in a critical situation IF
    there exists a pedestrian P AND
    P is in a crosswalk AND
    detection confidence C is greater than 90%"
